# 24/7 Global Support

The cell ships, the line ramps, and then the real work begins: keeping it running. Uptime is the metric that pays for the cell, and uptime is what we optimize for.

Every cell we ship comes with a remote monitoring dashboard, a 24/7 support contract, and on-site service in 14 countries. When something goes wrong, we know before you do — and we're already on it.

## What you get

- **Remote monitoring** — live cell health, cycle counts, error rates, and predictive alerts on a per-cell dashboard
- **24/7 phone & chat support** — with a 30-minute P1 response SLA
- **On-site service** — in 14 countries, with field engineers who know your cell
- **Spare parts** — pre-positioned inventory and 48-hour delivery on critical parts
- **Quarterly health audits** — on-site, with a written report and a forward-looking action list
- **Program backup** — every program versioned, off-site backed up, and restorable in minutes

## The bar

99.4% average uptime across all deployed cells. Not a marketing number — an actual measurement across the fleet. If your cell is below that, we own the gap.

## How escalation works

P1 (line down): 30-minute response, remote hands on first, on-site within SLA
P2 (degraded): 4-hour response, remote fix preferred
P3 (question / improvement): next business day

You always reach a human. You always reach someone who knows your cell. And you always have a path from "something's weird" to "fixed and root-caused."
