# Cell Design & 3D Simulation

The cell is the unit of automation. Everything we do — programming, vision, EOAT, safety — happens *inside* it. Get the cell wrong and the rest is fragile. Get it right and the rest is routine.

We design every cell in 3D before any steel is cut. Using the same simulation tools your operators will see on the floor (RoboDK, Visual Components, DELMIA), we prove out the cycle time, prove out the reach, and prove out the safety envelope — long before commissioning day.

## What we deliver

- **3D layout & simulation** — full cell in your software of choice, with all peripherals, fixtures, and operator zones modelled
- **Cycle-time study** — motion-profiled simulation with realistic accelerations, not theoretical max
- **Reach & singularity analysis** — every joint pose checked across the full work envelope
- **Collision detection** — between robot, EOAT, parts, fixtures, and operator hands
- **Offline programming** — programs written and validated in the sim, then exported to native code
- **Virtual commissioning** — PLC and safety logic tested before the cell is built

## The output

A cell that hits its cycle-time target on day one, not month three. A cell your team can re-configure without a service call. A cell that pays back on the timeline we modeled in the proposal.

## How we work

We start with your takt time, your floor constraints, and your operator workflow. We build the sim in week one, review it with your engineering team in week two, and freeze the design by week three. Programming and integration follow in parallel.
