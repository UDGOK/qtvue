# Vision & Sensing

The robot is the arm. Vision is the eyes. Get the eyes wrong and the arm is just expensive sculpture.

We integrate 2D and 3D vision, force/torque sensing, laser profiling, and proximity sensors — calibrated, tuned, and running reliably in production. We don't ship a vision job that works in the lab; we ship one that works at 3am on a Friday with the lights flickering.

## What we deliver

- **2D vision** — part detection, alignment, code reading, defect flagging
- **3D vision** — bin picking, tray picking, dimensional inspection, surface scan
- **Force/torque** — assembly insertion, polishing, deburring, compliance control
- **Laser profiling and LIDAR** — seam tracking for welding, volume measurement
- **Conveyor tracking** — dynamic pick from moving belts, encoded registration
- **Vision tuning** — lighting, optics, exposure, gain, job parameters — optimized for your parts

## The bar

A vision job that runs at 99%+ pass rate on real production parts, with cycle time under 200ms, that your maintenance team can re-tune when a supplier changes the box.

## How we work

We sample your actual parts in your actual lighting (not lab conditions), train the vision job on real reject/accept sets, and tune for the failure modes that bite in production — glare, occlusion, reflection, part-to-part variation. Then we hand off with full job documentation and a tuning SOP.
