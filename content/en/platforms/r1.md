# Unitree R1

The R1 is the lightweight, developer-friendly humanoid — the platform you hand
to a research student on day one. It's not a production robot; it's a learning
tool that ships with a real Unitree SDK and the same development model as the
G1 and H1.

## Where the R1 fits

- **STEM** — university robotics clubs, K-12 outreach, makerspaces.
- **HRI research** — human-robot interaction studies where you need a
  humanoid that fits in a lab.
- **Entry-level autonomy** — locomotion and basic manipulation research
  where the G1 would be overkill.

The R1 is the right answer when the question is "how do we get a humanoid
onto the lab bench this week" and not "how do we deploy a humanoid into
production." For the latter, see the H1 or H2.
