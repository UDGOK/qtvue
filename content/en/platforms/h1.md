# Unitree H1 / H1-2

The H1 is the full-size, fastest production humanoid on the market. 1.78 m tall,
3.7 m/s top speed, high-torque joint modules. It's the platform enterprise
R&D teams buy when the R1 or G1 are under-spec'd for the work.

## Where the H1 fits

- **Advanced research** in human-scale manipulation, dynamic locomotion,
  and high-speed control.
- **Enterprise R&D** where the budget is real and the use case is real —
  industrial human-robot coexistence pilots, heavy-payload manipulation
  research, demonstrator work for stakeholders.
- **Speed research** — the H1 is the only production humanoid that
  sprints. If your research depends on dynamic locomotion, this is the
  platform.

The H1 is also the most expensive platform in the Unitree range. We will
talk you out of buying one if a G1 or B2 will do the job.

## Programming the H1

Same `unitree_sdk2_python` as the rest of the family. The H1's `LocoClient`
exposes high-level locomotion modes (walk, run, sprint) plus per-joint
torque control for researchers who need to drop down a level.
