# Unitree H2 (new)

The H2 is the refined full-size humanoid — built on the lessons of the H1-2,
with unified onboard compute, stronger arms, and a meaningfully better
battery. Same approximate form factor; meaningfully upgraded internals.

## Where the H2 fits

The H2 is positioned as the demonstration and operator-in-the-loop research
platform in the Unitree humanoid line. Where the H1 was the "fastest," the
H2 is the "most capable in a lab."

If you're choosing between H1 and H2 today, the H2 is the better research
platform for new projects; the H1 still wins on raw sprint speed and is
cheaper. We'll help you pick in the scoping call.

## Programming the H2

Same `unitree_sdk2_python` as the rest of the family. The H2's unified
compute simplifies the SDK surface (one process, not three) and gives you
cleaner hooks for teleoperation and shared-autonomy research.
