# Unitree B2

The B2 is the industrial quadruped. Same DDS interface as the Go2, but built
for the field: 40 kg+ sustained payload, IP-rated, 4–6 hour battery under work.
If you need a robot to do a job in a place you don't want to send a person,
the B2 is the answer.

## Where the B2 fits

The B2 is over-spec'd for a research lab and exactly the right size for:

- **Industrial inspection** of substations, refineries, and chemical plants where
  a person goes on a daily walk-around. The B2 takes the walk and publishes
  telemetry.
- **Agriculture** for livestock monitoring, crop health, and perimeter patrol
  across uneven ground.
- **Construction and energy** for tool carriage and inspection on unstructured
  sites where a wheeled robot gets stuck.

The programming model is identical to the Go2 — same `unitree_sdk2_python` —
which means a team that knows the Go2 can ship a B2 deployment in days, not
weeks.
