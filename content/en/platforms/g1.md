# Unitree G1

The G1 is the humanoid platform that turned the "humanoid for research" category
from a six-figure line item into a realistic lab purchase. 23 DoF base, up to 43
DoF in the EDU configuration, dexterous hands. It runs the same Unitree SDK
as the rest of the family.

## Where the G1 fits

The G1 is the right platform when you need a full-body humanoid for **indoor
R&D** — university manipulation research, embodied-AI training, dexterous
manipulation prototyping. It is not an industrial product, and you should
treat its marketing materials as aspirational.

The honest callouts:

- It's **indoor only** — no IP rating, no outdoor deployment.
- The **2-hour real battery** matters for experiment planning; you budget
  charging time, not just run time.
- The 43-DoF **EDU** model is meaningfully different from the 23-DoF base.
  If you're doing manipulation work, you want the EDU.
- The default network configuration is **not safe for connected environments**.
  The UniPwn disclosure of 2025 affects the G1 over Bluetooth; we verify
  firmware and isolate the robot's network on every deployment.

If your use case is real-world manufacturing or service work, the G1 is
the wrong platform — and we'll tell you that in the scoping call.
